Feed My Goat

    Instruction:

  • Click Play button to start the game.
  • Click Watch Bot button to observe Bot play.
  • Use to move your goat around on the grid.
  • Tap space on your keyboard to pause/resume the game while playing.
  • Once you have cleared all the grass of the current grid, you'll proceed to the next levels where the goat's speed will be progressively higher in each.
  • Be careful not to lead your goat into the wolves / rocks or the game will be over !
